Turning a WAV / MP3 into 8-bit

A recording (WAV/MP3) has no note data, so first convert it to MIDI with a free audio-to-MIDI tool — Spotify basic-pitch works in the browser. Then use Convert a MIDI above. Simple melodies/leads convert best; busy mixes need cleanup.
